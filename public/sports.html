Cricket

As an Indian, it's practically impossible to escape the overwhelming presence of cricket in our culture. It's more than just a sport; it's almost a religion for many of us. I absolutely adore watching legends like Sachin Tendulkar, who's regarded as the epitome of batting brilliance, Rahul Dravid, known for his steadfast technique and sportsmanship, and Virat Kohli, who brings an electrifying energy to the game. They all inspire not only a love for the sport but also a sense of national pride.


Karate

Honestly, as a Black Belt Dan-1 myself, I see Karate as more than just a martial art; it's a form of meditation for me. The discipline required to master the techniques and the mental focus needed during practice really help me find balance and cultivate inner peace. Additionally, it teaches valuable life skills such as respect, perseverance, and confidence, making it a holistic practice that goes beyond just physical fitness
