"oui !! je parle aussi français"


"Yes !! I speak french too"


In 2015, I successfully completed the A1 level French course at Alliance Bhopal. I have consistently harbored an intrinsic curiosity regarding the acquisition of various languages. The experience proved to be immensely enriching, facilitating not only my linguistic development but also my understanding of the cultural practices intrinsic to the native speakers of the language.

I enjoy writing and listening poetry, specially Urdu poets


Building upon my passion for languages, I commenced the composition of short narratives in English during my early youth. This writing endeavor persisted throughout my collegiate experience, culminating in the creation of three short stories and a prodigious number of poems, the details of which have largely escaped my recollection.
